If you are running late

Able to proceed and complete the full session

  1. Call client (caller ID can be disabled if preferred) to inform them and provide your estimated time of arrival.
  2. No further action required.

Able to proceed and complete a partial session

  1. Call client (caller ID can be disabled if preferred) to inform them and provide your estimated time of arrival.
  2. Email accounts@hithrive.com.au and state the exact duration to be billed.

Notify the admin team ASAP or the full invoice may be automatically sent.

Unable to proceed

  1. Call the family and advise them of the situation. State clearly they will not be charged.
  2. Delete the appointment in Halaxy.
  3. Email accounts@hithrive.com.au to notify of the occurrence.

Notify the admin team ASAP or the full invoice may be automatically sent.

If client is running late
  1. Call client (caller ID can be disabled if preferred).
  2. Leave voicemail (state you will wait 15 minutes).
  3. Wait 2–3 minutes
  4. Call again - leave voicemail if possible.
  5. Wait until 15 minutes from session start time.

Arrives within the first 15 minutes

Session proceeds as per normal.

Arrives after the first 15 minutes

  • Session does not need to proceed (you may proceed at your discretion).
  • You may choose to use remaining time but you are not obligated to do so.

Our Service Agreement clearly states that if a client is more than 15 mins late, the full fee will be charged.

Follow the applicable 'No-show' process below.

No-show (home visit/telehealth)
  1. Ensure you have followed the 'If client is running late' process above.
  2. Session does not need to proceed (you may proceed at your discretion). You may choose to use the remaining time but you are not obligated to do so.

    Our Service Agreement clearly states that if a client is more than 15 mins late, the full fee will be charged.

  3. Email accounts@hithrive.com.au and include:
    • Client name
    • Date and time
    • Confirmation 2 calls were made
    • Confirmation 15 minutes waited

Record a clinical note

  1. Open appointment.
  2. Click pencil icon.
  3. Click Templates.
  4. Search “No-Show”.
  5. Select template.
  6. Edit details.
  7. Save & publish.

You must publish a note for a no-show.

No-show (school/kinder/daycare)
  1. Check in at reception and confirm child not on-site.
  2. Email accounts@hithrive.com.au and include:
    • Client name
    • Date and time
    • Reception confirmed child absent

Do not contact the parent - the admin team will handle follow-up.

Record a clinical note

  1. Open appointment.
  2. Click pencil icon.
  3. Click Templates.
  4. Search “No-Show”.
  5. Select template.
  6. Edit details.
  7. Save & publish.

You must publish a note for a no-show.

Extra time requested
  1. If you agree to stay longer, clearly state that the extra time is billable.
  2. Document verbal consent to be billed for extra time in the clinical note.
  3. Notify the admin team immediately via the 'Billing Requests' channel.

Invoices are automatically sent after session - additional time cannot be added once sent.

Failure to promptly notify the admin team may result in you not being paid for extra time.

Billing questions
  • Direct all billing queries to accounts@hithrive.com.au.
  • Do not negotiate fees on-site.
Cancellations
  • If a client attempts to cancel through you, direct them to hithrive.com.au/cancel
  • No cancellations will be processed outside this form.
Safety concerns
  • You must ensure you have read all risk assessments prior to attending premises.
  • It is your responsibility to ensure a risk assessment has been done for all locations you are attending.

Examples of unsafe behaviour

  • Aggressive or threatening behaviour.
  • Uncontrolled/aggressive animal.
  • Verbal abuse.
  • Physical intimidation.
  • Any situation that feels unsafe.

If you believe your safety or wellbeing is at risk

You have the right to:

  • Refuse to enter the premises.
  • Stop the session immediately.
  • Leave the premises.

A perceived threat is enough. You do not need proof of actual danger.

If you feel unsafe

  1. End the session immediately.
  2. State: “I am unable to continue due to safety concerns.”
  3. Leave the premises.
  4. Go to a safe location.
  5. Call Cristina (Director) on Microsoft Teams.
  6. If Cristina does not answer, call Clara (Senior Occupational Therapist).
  7. Complete a clinical note documenting the incident.

If in immediate danger, call emergency services.

Your safety comes first. Do not argue onsite - the appointment remains billable.
